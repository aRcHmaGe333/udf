# Support Universal Data Fabric

At Universal Data Fabric, we recognize the vital role that community, collaboration, and innovation play in shaping the future of technology. As we embark on this transformative journey, we invite interested parties—be it funders, sponsors, or partners—to join us in our mission. Your support is crucial for the advancement of Universal Data Fabric, and there are several exciting ways you can contribute:

### How You Can Support Us:
- **Funding Opportunities**: Consider providing financial support to help us develop and expand our capabilities. Your investment will directly impact the project’s success and sustainability.
- **Sponsorship**: Companies and organizations can sponsor specific initiatives or segments within the project. This is an excellent opportunity to align your brand with cutting-edge technology and innovation.
- **Partnership Discussions**: We are open to discussions on how we can collaborate and work together to achieve our goals. Your expertise and resources could complement our efforts in meaningful ways.

### Contact Us
We encourage you to reach out if you are interested in exploring funding or partnership options:
- **Open an Issue**: Feel free to create an issue in this repository to start a conversation.
- **Direct Contact**: You can also reach out directly to the project maintainers via email at support@udf.org.
- **Coalition or Discovery Calls**: We would love to schedule a coalition or discovery call to discuss how we can work together and the unique contributions you can offer. Please contact us to set this up at your earliest convenience.

Your involvement is pivotal, not just for the project but for the broader ecosystem we aim to build. Join us in supporting Universal Data Fabric and help shape the future of technology! We look forward to your connection!
